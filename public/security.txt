# Wellness Supplements Platform Security Policy

## Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

We take the security of our platform seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, 
please report it to us as soon as possible.

### How to Report
- Email: security@wellness-supplements.com
- Subject: [SECURITY] Brief description of the issue
- Include: Steps to reproduce, impact assessment, and any relevant details

### What to Include
1. A detailed description of the vulnerability
2. Steps to reproduce the issue
3. Potential impact and severity assessment
4. Any proof-of-concept code (if applicable)
5. Your contact information for follow-up

### Response Timeline
- Acknowledgment: Within 24 hours
- Initial assessment: Within 72 hours
- Resolution timeline: Based on severity (Critical: 24-48h, High: 1-2 weeks)

### Scope
This security policy applies to:
- wellness-supplements.com and all subdomains
- Mobile applications
- API endpoints
- Third-party integrations

### Out of Scope
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks against our facilities
- DoS/DDoS attacks
- Issues in third-party services beyond our control

### Responsible Disclosure
We ask that you:
- Give us reasonable time to address the issue before public disclosure
- Avoid accessing or modifying user data without permission
- Do not perform testing that could harm our users or services
- Report vulnerabilities as soon as you discover them

### Recognition
We appreciate security researchers who help improve our platform's security.
Qualified reports may be eligible for our Bug Bounty Program.

Thank you for helping keep our users' data safe!
